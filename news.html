JUNGLE KING - SUNJAH

Well long story short it’s a genre of electronic dance music which was a subgenre of hardcore. It was born in England in the early 90s. People that were marching towards colorful light future chose the happy hardcore path and people who lived in reality, the working class, they chose jungle, it was raw it was dark it was heavy it was most importantly real. Music itself is fast, contains breakbeat, dub style bassline, percussive loops, soundclash, riddim samples, synths, effects etc. Long story short it is a well known genre in the UK. This was an answer to USA hip hop scene at the time. I think it’s a gift to be a part of such maybe unique scene which in some countries is becoming a niche culture but in some it never even existed..

I was at a transition phase from heavy music scene to bits of electronic. Drum and bass was pretty popular at the time. After going to my first party (still remember like it was yesterday) and experiencing everything myself, straight away I knew this is where I see myself shining. I said to my friend at the time that somehow I don’t know how but we have to be a part of this. And this is how couple months later I’ve started DJ’ing in in 2012 and later on started making my own parties in 2014 with a group of people that I’ve gathered and called “Revolution Crew”. I think it was in 2014 that I’ve managed to get into an abandoned paper factory here in Aarhus Viby and made my first pirate party there. After that night the crew was born, and we’ve been active until around 2017 and at 2018 nothing was left. But I’m looking forward to the new crew to rise like a phoenix from revolution crew’s ashes and keep it coming.

Another heavy weight techno act on local ground. : : Sparatque

Ukrainian DJ and Producer Spartaque has become a name known for raw quality on the international techno landscape. He’s been at it for many years, but got his first break in 2007 DJing at Ukraine’s Global Gathering festival, and has continued with this festival every year since. He has added other vital performances to his resume by performing at Love Parade, Kazantip, Mayday, and others. Spartaque supplements these performances and maintains the momentum by way of his award-winning Supreme podcast, in addition to numerous guest mixes on other podcasts and radio shows.

Spartaque started out in his teenage years composing and producing dance music, finally realizing as a young adult the lucrative skill of DJing his works. His first piece to release was on a Virus Music album in 2005. A decade later, he now has over 300 released tracks on more than # labels. His arsenal includes both original works and remixes for some of the top artists around the world.

Maintaining balance between industry and artisanship, Spartaque has played a key role in the creation and development of several record labels, IAMT being the most highly regarded in the world of techno music. He has been nominated and awarded for his many achievements in the past decade, proving that ever since his modest start in 2005, he has become an influential artist in the world of techno.
