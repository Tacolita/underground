ABOUT US

Aarhus underground – an English writing publication based in Aarhus, Denmark. It is an independent online magazine for all alternative music lovers. We provide you a wide range of different music events, relevant articles about labels and artists, as well as interviews and recommendations to introduce you to the Underground culture of Aarhus.

Our main focus – introduce you to a different sounds and environments that are based in Aarhus. We want you to feel the joy of discovering new scenes, new sounds and new people you didn’t even knew existed..

Let’s dive in, in the new experiences. Join us now!
